About

Know Your Government is an attempt to raise awareness about political leaders in India in common person.

Our Information Base

We database currently has information of elected Members of Parliaments and Chief Ministers of different States

For Collecting Information & Stats we referred to : Lok Sabha Portal - India 60 - My Neta Info

How to Use It?

You can go ahead and type the name of a the politican or you can use location services to track leaders nearby your location. In location service, you can, enter the name of your region / connect this application with Google+ to help us track your past locations / Share your current location if your browser supports geolocation

Politician Information

On a politican's page, you can view basic information about him/her, updates about their activities from twitter, recent news updates involving them and finally a sentiment analysis. Sentiment analysis is done on weighted factors involving updates from social circles, news articles from national dailies(currently TOI and Hindu) and Reader comments on those Websites
Currently we have data only for major politicians elected in power.
